# Success Stories – Real People Helped

*These are shared anonymously with permission.*

---

## Story 1: Stopped a SIM Swap
A volunteer warned his father about SIM swap attacks. Weeks later, his father got a strange text saying his SIM was being activated on another device. Remembering the warning, he immediately called his phone company and stopped the swap. His bank account stayed safe.

---

## Story 2: Grandma Didn't Click
After a workshop at a local community center, an older woman received a text saying her "package couldn't be delivered." She almost clicked, but remembered the "check the link" tip. She deleted it instead.

---

## Story 3: Small Business Saved
A volunteer shared the `fake-tech-support.md` warning with a local shop owner. Two weeks later, the shop owner got a call from "Microsoft" saying his computer had a virus. He hung up and avoided paying $500 in gift cards.
