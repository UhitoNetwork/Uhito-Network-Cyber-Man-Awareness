## üìã **VOLUNTEER REQUIREMENTS**

### Minimum to Start:
1. **Age**: 18+ (21+ for certain activities)
2. **Background**: Clean legal record
3. **Commitment**: Minimum 6 months
4. **Time**: 5+ hours per week
5. **Mindset**: Safety-first, ego-last

### Progressive Requirements:
- **Level 1**: Basic safety training
- **Level 2**: Communication certification
- **Level 3**: Technical certification (for technical help)
- **Level 4**: Liability insurance (for community work)

---

## ‚öñÔ∏è **LEGAL REALITIES**

### Without Certification:
- You can ONLY help immediate family
- NO public advice or assistance
- NO community workshops
- NO business consultations
- **Risk**: Personal liability for any harm

### With Certification:
- LIMITED community help allowed
- REQUIRED liability insurance
- MUST follow professional standards
- REGULAR legal compliance checks
- **Protection**: Some legal coverage
