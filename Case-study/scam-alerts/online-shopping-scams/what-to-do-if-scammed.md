# ⚠️ What to Do If You Get Scammed

## Step by Step Guide

---

### 🚨 Immediately (First 24 Hours)

#### Step 1: 🛑 Stop Talking
- Don't argue with scammer
- Don't send more money
- Don't threaten them

#### Step 2: 🏦 Contact Your Bank
- Call bank immediately
- Ask to freeze or reverse payment
- Say: "I was scammed, please help"
- Note down complaint number

> *"Banks can only help if you act fast. Every minute matters."*

#### Step 3: 📸 Save Everything
- Screenshots of conversation
- Screenshots of payment
- Screenshots of seller profile
- Phone number used
- Date and time of calls

---

### 📞 Get Call Records

If you called the seller:

1. Go to **mobile provider office** (Jio, Airtel, etc)
2. Take **police complaint copy**
3. Ask for **call details** of that date/time
4. Get **official document**
5. **Make a copy** before sharing

> *"Call records can reveal who the scammer really is."*

---

### 👮 Report to Police

Even if they say "can't help":

- ✅ File a complaint anyway
- ✅ Get written proof
- ✅ Take complaint number
- ✅ Use it for telecom records

---
