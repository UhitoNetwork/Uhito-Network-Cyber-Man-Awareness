# 🛡️ How to Protect Yourself from Online Shopping Scams

## Simple Tips - No Tech Words

---

### ✅ Before You Buy

| Do | Don't |
|-----|-------|
| 🧾 Ask for receipt or invoice | ❌ Pay full price before seeing item |
| 📞 Video call to verify seller | ❌ Trust "I'll send my partner" stories |
| 🏦 Use payment with protection | ❌ Use bank transfer to strangers |
| 👥 Check seller reviews | ❌ Believe "too good to be true" deals |
| 📍 Meet in public place | ❌ Go alone to isolated areas |

---

### 📞 Verify the Seller

Ask these questions:
- "Can we video call so I see the item?"
- "Do you have original bill?"
- "Why are you selling?"
- "Can someone come with me to see it?"

If they avoid answering → **Red flag** 🚩

---

### 📱 Safe Payment Methods

| Safe ✅ | Risky ❌ |
|---------|----------|
| Cash on delivery | Bank transfer |
| PayPal (goods & services) | UPI to unknown |
| Escrow services | Gift cards |
| Credit card (can dispute) | Cryptocurrency |

---

### 🧠 Trust Your Gut

If something feels wrong:
- 🛑 Stop talking
- 🚶 Walk away
- 📞 Ask a friend's opinion
- 🔍 Search seller name online

> *"Scammers rush you. Legit sellers let you think."*

---

### 📲 Protect Your Number

- Don't use main SIM for unknown deals
- Use Truecaller to check numbers
- Block spam callers immediately

---

### 📢 Share This With

- 👪 Family (especially elders)
- 👫 Friends buying online
- 🧑‍🎓 Young people new to shopping
