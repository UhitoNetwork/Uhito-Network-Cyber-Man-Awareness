# Trusted Links for Reporting Scams and Getting Help

## If You've Been Scammed
- **In the US:** report to [FTC](https://reportfraud.ftc.gov)
- **In the UK:** report to [Action Fraud](https://www.actionfraud.police.uk)
- **In Canada:** report to [Canadian Anti‑Fraud Centre](https://www.antifraudcentre-centreantifraude.ca)
- **In Australia:** report to [Scamwatch](https://www.scamwatch.gov.au)

## Check if Your Email/Password Has Been Leaked
- [Have I Been Pwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com) – enter your email to see if it's in a data breach.

## Learn More (Simple Guides)
- [Cyber.gov.au](https://www.cyber.gov.au) – Australian government advice
- [StaySafeOnline.org](https://staysafeonline.org) – US nonprofit
- [GetSafeOnline.org](https://www.getsafeonline.org) – UK advice

## Free Security Tools
- **Password Manager:** [Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com) (free)
- **2FA App:** [Google Authenticator](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.authenticator2) (free) or [Microsoft Authenticator](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/mobile-authenticator-app) (free)
- **VPN:** [ProtonVPN](https://protonvpn.com) (free tier)
