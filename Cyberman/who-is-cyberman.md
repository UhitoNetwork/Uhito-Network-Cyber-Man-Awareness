# WHO IS CYBER-MAN?

## The Simple Truth

Cyber-Man is someone who knows about digital dangers and explains them in simple words so everyone can protect themselves.

### They Are:
- A person who learned cybersecurity properly (with certificates)
- Someone who translates tech threats into everyday warnings
- A community protector who helps without confusing people
- Proof that skills can be used for good, not harm

### They Are NOT:
- A hacker or vigilante
- A government agent
- Someone without proper certification
- A technical expert who speaks in confusing terms

### Their Core:
They took their knowledge (from certificates like Security+, CEH, etc.) and use it to:
1. Warn community about scams
2. Teach simple protection methods
3. Help without breaking laws
4. Make security understandable for all
